I am Shannen Mirarchi and I am a bilingual reporter and anchor for Noticias WUFT. I a student at the University of Florida, studying Telecommunications and I have a great passion to tell the stories of others. Those in need to be heard and those in need to have the light shine on them. I am Argentine and Cuban with a Spanish and Italian heritage. I love my diverse cultural background and I pride myself on the fact that I am Latina and bilingual. It’s a blessing and I am so ready to graduate and conquer the world.

My dream is to work for the biggest Spanish Network right now, Univision. I interned for them my freshman year of college. I had the best experience with them that allowed me to know for sure what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. We worked with a show that was titled “Ahora en Nuestra Comunidad” which is translated to “Now in Our Community” and it basically featured stories about average people in our community that have struggles like any average person and are featured for the world to see. I also had a life-changing experience with StoryCorps.
